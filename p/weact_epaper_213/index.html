2.13" WeAct E-Paper Module (SSD1680, 122x250, SPI)

WeAct Studio 2.13 inch black/white e-paper module (SSD1680 driver, 122x250, YRD0213 panel), SPI via a 2x4 (dual-row!) 2.54 header near the left edge (1 BUSY 2 RES 3 D/C 4 CS 5 SCL 6 SDA 7 GND 8 VCC, odd pins in the edge-side column), 72x30mm board, four 3.2mm holes. The official KiCad library has no e-paper content at all.
